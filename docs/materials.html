Material Library

A number of materials are available for use as shielding, source material, and for buildup factors. Physical data for each material is stored in materialLibrary.yml.

Photon cross sections in the form of mass attenuation coefficients are available for elements with atomic numbers from 1 (Hydrogen) to 100 (Fermium). Selected additional sheilding materials are also provided, including:

  • Aluminum, 2.7 g/cm3

  • Iron, 7.874 g/cm3

  • Lead, 11.35 g/cm3

  • Air, 0.001205 g/cm3

  • Concrete, 2.3 g/cm3

  • Water, 1.0 g/cm3

  • SS304L, 8.0 g/cm3

  • UO2, 10.412 g/cm3 (95% of theoretical density)

  • Ion Exchange Resin, 2.01 g/cm3 (modeled as a mixture of water (6.47 wt%) in concrete (93.53 wt%))

Buildup factors are included in the library for the following materials. Additional buildup factors may be added to the library, while using an existing buildup factor with an atomic number close to that of the shield is usually sufficient.
